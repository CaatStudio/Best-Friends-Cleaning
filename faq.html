Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all our own professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. Our team comes fully equipped with everything needed to clean your space thoroughly. We use high-quality, effective cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets.

Are your products eco-friendly?

We prioritize the health of your family and the environment. We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the planet. Our green cleaning solutions are just as effective as traditional cleaners but without the harmful chemicals.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home during the cleaning. Many of our clients prefer to leave us a key or provide access instructions so they can go about their day while we work. Our team is fully bonded and insured, and we treat your home with the utmost respect and care.

How do I schedule or cancel an appointment?

Scheduling is easy! You can call us at 801-831-5004, email us at bestfriendscleaningcompany@gmail.com, or fill out our contact form. For cancellations or rescheduling, we ask for at least 24 hours notice to avoid any fees. We understand that life happens and we'll work with you to find a convenient time.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our top priority! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with our service, please contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean any areas of concern at no additional charge. We're not satisfied until you are!
