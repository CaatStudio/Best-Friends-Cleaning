About us

We are committed to providing the best service you deserve!

Meet Milly and Ruben

When we first arrived in the United States, Mildred found a job cleaning homes. It was difficult at first, but over time she realized she had a natural talent for attention to detail and customer service. Together, we decided to start our own business: Best Friends Cleaning Company, LLC.

What started as a necessity became our passion. We believe that a clean space is more than just aesthetics—it's about creating an environment where families can thrive, where guests feel welcome, and where people can truly relax.

Our Story

The journey that led us to become Utah's trusted cleaning professionals

2010 - New Beginnings

Milly and Ruben arrived in the United States with big dreams and determination. Milly found her first job cleaning homes, discovering her natural talent for detail and customer service.

2015 - The Vision

After years of working for others, Milly and Ruben realized they could provide better, more personalized service. They decided to start their own cleaning company focused on quality and customer satisfaction.

2016 - Best Friends Cleaning is Born

Best Friends Cleaning Company, LLC was officially founded. The name reflects our philosophy: we treat every client like a best friend, with care, respect, and attention to their unique needs.

2024 - Growing Strong

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers across the Wasatch Front. Our commitment to eco-friendly products, reliable service, and exceptional results has made us a trusted name in Utah cleaning services.
